BLOOD GROUP TESTING

What is your blood type?

Blood Group Information

Blood groups are classified based on the presence or absence of specific antigens on the surface of red blood cells.

  • A+: Has A antigen on red blood cells and Rh antigen on the surface.
  • A-: Has A antigen on red blood cells but no Rh antigen.
  • B+: Has B antigen on red blood cells and Rh antigen on the surface.
  • B-: Has B antigen on red blood cells but no Rh antigen.
  • AB+: Has both A and B antigens on red blood cells and Rh antigen on the surface.
  • AB-: Has both A and B antigens on red blood cells but no Rh antigen.
  • O+: Has no A or B antigens on red blood cells but has Rh antigen on the surface.
  • O-: Has no A or B antigens on red blood cells and no Rh antigen.
